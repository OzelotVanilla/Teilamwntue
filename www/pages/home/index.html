What's Teilamwntue?

Defination

Teilamwntue is a language used by the tribe Teilamwnto lived in High Land Teilamwn.

History

There were an ancient land called High Land Teilamwn, which is not only cold but also lack of nature resources. In this land, a new tribe appeared. Their name is Teilamwnto.

Teilamwntue is the product of cold. The people who once survived from the Sevoné created Teilamwn, along with Teilamwntue. Sevoné, which is known as the biggest disaster affecting every customs of Teilamwnto, caused a wide-range damage to every corner of the Land, making all things frozen.

---- Annie Dailen

The ancestors of Teilamwnto were survived. They then try everything they can do to fight against the freezing cold weather.

In the end they made it, along with the improvement they made in their unique custom.
